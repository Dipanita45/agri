# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to Fasal Saathi.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started
- Provide and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other community members

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Offensive comments or actions
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any other conduct that could be considered unprofessional

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces and when representing the project.

## Questions

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please contact us at support@fasalsaathi.com.
